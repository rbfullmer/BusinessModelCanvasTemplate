The Business Model Canvas

Key Partners

Police Department - problems in neighborhood (break ins, vandalism in neighborhood)
Fire Department - fires in the area
City and State Departments - emergency systems
KSL Classifieds - adds of neighbors

Key Activities

Keep people up to date with concerns in their neighborhood
Revenue through advertisments on website
Keep current on trends so customers are comfortable using the website

Value Proposition

Alerts for fire, hazards, break ins, vandalism for the neighborhood
Information that concerns the neighborhood
Networking of neighborhood

Customer Relationship

Help with problems in setting software for neighborhood
Setting up new nodes that can be customized for a particulr need

Customer Segments

Money is generated by advertisments
cities, neighborhoods are our customers and with a great product advertising will be generated

Key Resources

Customer relations is key to provide a great product
feedback and support of customers will make the website better
emergency supply stores and the like will help in establishing a basis for what to put on the website

Channels

Any emergency that affects their neighborhood
Awareness through emergency preparedness stores, neighborhood watch meetings and media coverage

Cost Structure

Costs of salaries, advertisement, building rent
developing a viable product that will work with different neighborhoods but be interchangable from one neighborhood to the next

Revenue Streams

Revenue is generated through advertising on the website and not through users
