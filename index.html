The Business Model Canvas

Key Partners

  • Current HOA's
  • Neighborhood Watch Groups

    • Police Department - problems in neighborhood (break ins, vandalism in neighborhood) Fire Department - fires in the area City and State Departments - emergency systems KSL Classifieds - adds of neighbors

Key Activities

  • Notifications
  • Alerts
  • Revenue comes in the form of very specific advertising to the neighboorhood and uders

    • Keep people up to date with concerns in their neighborhood Revenue through advertisments on website Keep current on trends so customers are comfortable using the website

Value Proposition

  • Have links to Homes and Cars for sale in the neighborhood
  • Saves Money by protecting from theft and emergency
  • Brings neighborhoods together
  • Promotes safety and communication

    • Alerts for fire, hazards, break ins, vandalism for the neighborhood Information that concerns the neighborhood Networking of neighborhood

Customer Relationship

  • Customers would have very little direct communication with the owners/developers, however the site would be very simple and self explanatory, allowing for simple set up and satisfied users.

    • Help with problems in setting software for neighborhood Setting up new nodes that can be customized for a particulr need

Customer Segments

  • Customers need to be able to log in and set up their notifications easily
  • Users would rely on the group and others in their neighborhood to make the site successful.

    • Money is generated by advertisments cities, neighborhoods are our customers and with a great product advertising will be generated

Key Resources

  • Users may need to be notified if their group has not logged on in over a week or a month, as the site's benefit and success depends on the neighborhood users.

    • Customer relations is key to provide a great product feedback and support of customers will make the website better emergency supply stores and the like will help in establishing a basis for what to put on the website

Channels

  • Social Networking will be a major assest in the website.
  • Satisfied users will speak publicly about their new found security and neighborhood connection

    • Any emergency that affects their neighborhood Awareness through emergency preparedness stores, neighborhood watch meetings and media coverage

Cost Structure

  • Membership Would be Free
  • Time available will be the deciding cost for each member of the group.
  • Website domain, hardware, server/site hosting, will carry a cost.

Costs of salaries, advertisement, building rent developing a viable product that will work with different neighborhoods but be interchangable from one neighborhood to the next

Revenue Streams

  • Car and Classified websites will be able to have custom advertisements of listing in the neighborhood of the group, making sales much more convienent and local
  • Local Realtors would be able to post their picture, ad, or bio, for when the user is interested in selling their home or moving.
  • Insurance companies would advertise on the site, for renter's and property insurance, and auto insurance. They could offer competitive discounts to the users, since the site is technically providing better security for their property.

    • Revenue is generated through advertising on the website and not through users
